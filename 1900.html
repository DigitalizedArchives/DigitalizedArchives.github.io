Overview

The 1900s was a decade marked by significant social and technological changes. This page highlights key events from the decade.

Major Events

  • 1901: The first Nobel Prizes are awarded.
  • 1903: The Wright brothers achieve the first powered flight.
  • 1905: Albert Einstein publishes his theory of relativity.
  • 1906: The San Francisco earthquake occurs.
  • 1908: The Model T is introduced by Henry Ford.

Pop Culture

The 1900s was an exciting time for art, music, and literature. Notable highlights include:

  • Popular authors: Mark Twain, Henry James, and Jack London.
  • Music trends: The rise of ragtime and early jazz.

Technology

The decade saw rapid advancements in technology, significantly shaping the modern world:

  • The introduction of electric streetcars in cities.
  • Advancements in medicine, including the use of anesthesia and antiseptics.
