Decade: 1160

Overview

The 1160s saw the continued impact of the Crusades on European politics and society, along with significant cultural developments.

Key Events

  • 1163: The construction of Notre-Dame Cathedral begins in Paris.
  • 1164: The first recorded instance of the use of the crossbow in warfare in France.
  • 1165: The birth of Richard the Lionheart, a key figure in the Crusades.
  • 1169: Saladin captures Jerusalem, prompting the Third Crusade.
