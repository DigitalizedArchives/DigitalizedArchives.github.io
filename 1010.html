1010

Overview

The year 1010 saw the continued influence of the Vikings in Europe and the expansion of the Holy Roman Empire under Emperor Henry II.

Key Events

  • Viking Settlements: The Vikings continued to establish settlements in parts of England and Ireland, influencing local cultures.
  • Henry II of Germany: Henry II becomes Holy Roman Emperor, strengthening central authority in the empire.
  • Construction of St. Peter's Basilica: The original St. Peter's Basilica in Rome is rebuilt during this period.
  • Song Dynasty Advances: The Song Dynasty in China continues to innovate in technology, commerce, and culture.
