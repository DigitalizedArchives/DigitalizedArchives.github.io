1980

Overview

The year 1980 marked significant events in history, including advancements in technology and major political changes.

Key Events

  • Mount St. Helens Eruption: The eruption of Mount St. Helens in Washington, USA, devastated the surrounding region.
  • Solidarity Movement in Poland: The Solidarity labor movement begins in Poland, challenging communist rule.
  • Ronald Reagan Elected President: Ronald Reagan is elected as the 40th president of the United States.
  • U.S. Boycotts Moscow Olympics: The U.S. leads a boycott of the 1980 Moscow Olympics in protest of the Soviet invasion of Afghanistan.
