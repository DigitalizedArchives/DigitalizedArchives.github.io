1910

Overview

The year 1910 was marked by significant advancements in technology and culture, including the first public radio broadcasts and notable events in the lead-up to World War I.

Key Events

  • First Radio Broadcast: The first public radio broadcast occurs in 1910, paving the way for mass communication.
  • Women Suffrage Movement: The movement gains momentum in several countries, advocating for women's right to vote.
  • First Airplane Flight: The Wright brothers continue their advancements in aviation, making significant flights in 1910.
  • Mexico's Revolution: The Mexican Revolution continues, leading to significant changes in the country's political landscape.
