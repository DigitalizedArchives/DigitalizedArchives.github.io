Decade: 1190

Overview

The decade of the 1190s was significant for political changes and cultural developments, including the rise of universities and advancements in trade.

Key Events

  • 1190: The Third Crusade begins, with major leaders like Richard the Lionheart and Philip II of France participating.
  • 1191: Siege of Acre concludes, marking a crucial victory for the Crusaders.
  • 1192: Richard I returns to England, having established a truce with Saladin.
  • 1199: Death of Richard I, leading to political instability in England.
