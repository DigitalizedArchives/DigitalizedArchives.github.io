2000

Overview

The year 2000 marked the turn of the millennium, accompanied by significant events in technology, politics, and culture.

Key Events

  • Y2K Bug: Fears of a global computer malfunction as the year 2000 arrives.
  • Dot-com Bubble Burst: The collapse of internet-based companies after a speculative boom.
  • Vladimir Putin Becomes President: Vladimir Putin becomes the President of Russia, beginning his long political tenure.
  • Sydney Olympics: The 2000 Summer Olympics take place in Sydney, Australia, drawing international attention.
