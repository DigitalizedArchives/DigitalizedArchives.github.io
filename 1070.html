Decade: 1070

Overview

The decade of the 1070s was marked by further developments in feudalism and ongoing conflicts in Europe.

Key Events

  • 1071: The Battle of Manzikert results in a significant victory for the Seljuk Turks.
  • 1077: The Walk to Canossa, where Henry IV seeks forgiveness from Pope Gregory VII.
  • 1073: Pope Gregory VII begins his reforms of the Church, known as the Gregorian Reforms.
  • 1075: The Investiture Controversy begins, a conflict between church and state over the appointment of bishops.
