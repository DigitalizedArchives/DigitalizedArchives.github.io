1990

Overview

The year 1990 witnessed transformative global events, including political shifts and the rise of technology.

Key Events

  • Fall of the Berlin Wall: The reunification of Germany following the fall of the Berlin Wall in 1989.
  • Nelson Mandela Released: Nelson Mandela is released after 27 years of imprisonment in South Africa.
  • Hubble Space Telescope Launched: NASA launches the Hubble Space Telescope, opening new windows to the universe.
  • Gulf War Begins: Iraq invades Kuwait, leading to the first Gulf War.
