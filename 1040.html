Decade: 1040

Overview

The 1040s were marked by significant political changes and developments in various regions across Europe.

Key Events

  • 1040: Battle of Carham, a significant conflict in Scottish history.
  • 1042: Edward the Confessor becomes King of England.
  • 1045: The first Norman castles are built in England.
  • 1046: The death of Pope Clement II.
