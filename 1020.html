Decade: 1020

Overview

The decade of the 1020s saw continued growth in trade and culture in Europe, as well as notable military conflicts.

Key Events

  • 1021: Construction of Santiago de Compostela Cathedral begins.
  • 1025: Byzantine Empire gains control over much of the Balkans.
  • 1026: First recorded mention of the city of Poznań in Poland.
  • 1029: Treaty of Barcelona is signed, establishing peace between the Crown of Aragon and the Kingdom of Navarre.
