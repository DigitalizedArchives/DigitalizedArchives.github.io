Decade: 1060

Overview

The 1060s were characterized by various political shifts and cultural developments across Europe.

Key Events

  • 1066: The Norman Conquest of England, marking a significant change in the country’s governance.
  • 1065: Westminster Abbey is consecrated, becoming a significant religious site.
  • 1069: The Harrying of the North occurs, a campaign led by William the Conqueror.
  • 1064: The start of the construction of the Tower of London.
