2020

Overview

The year 2020 was defined by the global pandemic of COVID-19, which impacted daily life and economies worldwide, alongside significant social movements and political changes.

Key Events

  • COVID-19 Pandemic: The outbreak of COVID-19 led to global health crises and lockdowns.
  • Black Lives Matter Protests: Protests erupted across the world following the death of George Floyd.
  • US Presidential Election: The election of Joe Biden marked a significant political shift in the United States.
  • Global Economic Recession: Many countries faced economic downturns due to the pandemic.
