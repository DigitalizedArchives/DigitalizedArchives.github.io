Decade: 900

Overview

The 900s marked a period of political instability in Europe and the rise of regional powers.

Key Events

  • 901: The establishment of the kingdom of Navarra.
  • 907: The Battle of Tettenhall occurs in England.
  • 909: The founding of the Abbey of Cluny in France.
  • 910: The Battle of Pavia takes place.
