1930

Overview

The year 1930 was marked by the Great Depression, which had significant effects on economies worldwide.

Key Events

  • Stock Market Crash: The Great Depression begins with the stock market crash of 1929, impacting economies worldwide.
  • Dust Bowl: Severe drought and poor agricultural practices lead to a devastating Dust Bowl in the US.
  • Rise of Fascism: Fascist regimes gain power in several countries, including Germany and Italy.
  • World War II Begins: Tensions escalate in Europe, leading to the outbreak of World War II in 1939.
