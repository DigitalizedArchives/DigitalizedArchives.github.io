1950

Overview

The 1950s saw the early stages of the Cold War, the Korean War, and the rise of consumerism and television culture.

Key Events

  • Korean War Begins: The Korean War breaks out as North Korea invades South Korea.
  • Brown v. Board of Education: The U.S. Supreme Court rules that racial segregation in schools is unconstitutional.
  • McCarthyism: U.S. Senator Joseph McCarthy leads a campaign against alleged communists in the U.S. government.
  • Sputnik Launched: The Soviet Union launches Sputnik, the first artificial Earth satellite, sparking the Space Race.
